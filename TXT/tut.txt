-till level 60 kill dragons and a few dragon lords. frost dragons are also good
-around 70 killing heroes is interesting
-For a lvl 70, behemoth may prove challenging
-lvl 70, small hellhounds are well worth it
-lvl 73, djinns are easy, yet a bit rewarding
-lvl 75 killing djinns I have noticed that it is good to carry two wands to compensate for the imunities that certain creatures have
-ghastly dragons are quite hard at lvl 70, and the dont give to much exp. Not recommended
-lvl 78, brain death is hard.
-lvl 80, dragon mix is a good place for hunting
-lvl 80, ice witches are easy and give good exp
-lvl 80 nightmares may prove challenging if your alone, but the give good exp
-lvl 85, icy dragons are easy and give loads of exp, just bomb SD
-lvl 90, madusas and serpent spawns are a little difficult but good exp
-lvl 100, still hunting icy dragons, still yield good exp
-lvl 100, I killed a demon, it took long but I got some good exp
-lvl 100, defilers are not worth the trouble. Not recommended
-the imperor, lvl 100, pain in the butt, the become invisible
-sea serpends(sea king) are not too hard and yield good exp, lvl 100
-lvl 100, wyrms are good, but dont yield that much exp
-lvl 100, hellspawns are great, good exp and not too hard
lvl 120, hydras are good, not too hard, and decent exp
-lvl 130, plaguesmiths are not too hard but dont yield good exp
-lvl 130, martes 13 are hard
